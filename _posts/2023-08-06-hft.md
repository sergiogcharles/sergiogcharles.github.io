In this post, I will describe an approach to high-frequency trading, whereby we optimize the following objective:
\begin{equation}
\mathbb{E}_{\delta_t^a,\delta_t^b}[U(W_T)]
\end{equation}

whereby $W_t=X_t+Q_tS_t$ is the wealth of the trader, $X_t$ is the cash, and $Q_t$ is the quantity of the risky-asset (stock) $S_t$.
