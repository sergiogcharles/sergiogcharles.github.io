Sergio Charles

I’m a co-founder of Thesis, an applied AI lab building the platform to accelerate machine learning. I previously worked at Google X and NVIDIA, and in the Stanford AI Lab with Chelsea Finn and Andrew Ng. I earned an M.S. in Statistics and a B.S. in Mathematics and Computer Science from Stanford. My prior work spans pure math, reinforcement learning, and natural language processing, from neural machine translation to autonomous RL for robotics.
